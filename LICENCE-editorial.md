## What this means in practice

Editorial content **cannot** be republished, mirrored, or used to train
machine-learning systems without explicit written permission from the
project operator.

Editorial content **can** be:

- Quoted briefly with attribution, under fair-dealing principles
- Linked to
- Cited in academic work, journalism, or commentary
- Used as inspiration or reference for original work in a fork

## The fork distinction

When forking this project for another jurisdiction (see `FORKING.md`):

- The **schema, scripts, methodology, design system, and structural
  approach** are CC-BY-4.0 and free to reuse.
- The **data** is CC-BY-4.0 and free to reuse with attribution.
- The **editorial content** of CanadaScanada is NOT licensed for reuse.
  Your fork must author its own About page, its own Methodology page,
  its own explainer text, and its own brand identity. Use the upstream
  as a model, not as a content source.
